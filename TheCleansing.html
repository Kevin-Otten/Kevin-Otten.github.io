The Game


The game is a ability based shooter with a grapple hook where the goal is to clear the levels and the boss that resides in them. The basic story is that you are a reaper who's job it is to end the demonic incursion into the world of the living.

My contributions


Videos showing my prototyped height map generations that happens on construction (before play). Inspiration of this comes from a presentation I found on how horizon zero dawn handled its air-based enemy. Its a simplified version but it works. The AI itself has a few checks to decide on what height to fly. Mainly the players height, a check close in front of it and a check further away from it. Combined these checks should stop it from running into walls and other objects while staying at the preferred distance and height from the player.
