The Game


The game is a 2.5D shooter where you can transfer to an enemy’s body. While the enemies themselves are all the same in behaviour they have a rock paper scissors weakness system based on their colour. As already mentioned you can switch bodies with enemies leaving your own to be taken over by the AI. This is useful to pick a healthier and potentially stronger body for taking out the remaining enemies.

The game can be found on Itch.io through this link.
